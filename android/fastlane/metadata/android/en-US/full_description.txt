HankoHanko is a smart stitch counting app designed for knitters and crocheters.

Key Features

Voice-Activated Counting
Count stitches hands-free with voice commands like "one stitch", "two stitch". Keep your focus on your craft!

Multiple Project Management
Manage all your ongoing knitting projects in one place. Each project maintains its own independent counter and notes.

Row Counter
Automatically reset the count and increment the row number for each row. Easily track complex patterns.

Notification Settings
Receive alerts when you reach your target stitch count. Supports vibration and voice feedback.

Dark Mode
Comfortable dark mode for nighttime crafting sessions.

Free with Ads
All basic features are free to use. Support app development by watching ads.

Enjoy more convenient and enjoyable knitting time with HankoHanko!
